Thuna activities

Besides providing information about the disease, Thuna currently concentrate at providing food at the time of the campaign.This is because there is a high chance for the state to face starvation due to lack of availability of food.
As the part of the campaign and the state has gone to a complete shut down no one is allowed to get out to purchase things.Thus there is lack of availability of food which is the basic need for ones survival.

#donate-and-feed

There are various clubs and communities all over the state that are willing to provide food some of them are community kitchens initiated at each districts.
What Thuna does is that ,it provides the details of such communities in each district and locality.The details include their number and region it covers.Thuna also provides a form for the person to fill if the person requires our help to get the food.
