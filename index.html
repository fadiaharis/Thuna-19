What we are..

Thuna is basically an organisation formed by few youngsters at the time when corona (COVID-19) outbreak started in the state of kerala.Initially we focused at providing proper assistance on how to face the disease by taking guidance and tips from expert doctors. We also pointed out the symptoms that a person affected with corona virus faces.We also provide help desk to clear their doubts on desease for those who are in quarentine.Thuna was also active in providing latest updates from all over the world on COVID-19.

Our team also succeeded in providing proper isolation facities. Thuna which was formed from about few people now turned out to be an active community.It's an humanitarian organisation
